:::tip Choosing Your Distribution

**New to Linux?** ‚Üí Ubuntu or Debian  
**Enterprise Environment?** ‚Üí RHEL, Rocky Linux, or AlmaLinux  
**Containers & Docker?** ‚Üí Alpine Linux or CoreOS  
**Latest Software?** ‚Üí Fedora or Arch  
**Security Testing?** ‚Üí Kali Linux or Parrot OS  
**Maximum Security?** ‚Üí Qubes OS  
**Performance Focused?** ‚Üí Clear Linux or Gentoo  
**Virtualization Host?** ‚Üí Proxmox VE  
**Learning RHEL?** ‚Üí Rocky Linux or AlmaLinux  
**Maximum Control?** ‚Üí Arch or Gentoo  
**No Systemd?** ‚Üí Void Linux or Alpine  
**Reproducible Systems?** ‚Üí NixOS

:::
